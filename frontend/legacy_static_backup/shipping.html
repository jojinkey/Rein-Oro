Shipping

1. Dispatch Overview

At Rein Oro, we understand that prompt and secure shipping is an essential element of a luxury experience. Every order is handled with the utmost care, hand-packed in clean facilities, and dispatched using premium delivery partners.

We process all orders within 24 to 48 business hours of purchase verification. Orders placed on Sundays or national holidays will be processed on the next business day.

2. Rates & Timelines

We offer shipping options designed to fit your convenience. Shipping charges and delivery estimates depend on your cart size and method selected during checkout:

Method Order Value Cost Est. Delivery
Standard Shipping Below ₹999 ₹99 4 - 6 Business Days
Standard Shipping ₹999 and above FREE 4 - 6 Business Days
Express Shipping Any value ₹149 2 - 3 Business Days

Free Shipping Threshold

Enjoy free Standard Shipping on all orders of ₹999 or more. This discount is automatically calculated and applied to your order subtotal during checkout, with no coupon code required.

3. Safe Packaging

To preserve natural crunch, taste, and nutrition, all Rein Oro Makhanas and premium dry fruits are packed inside hygienically sealed containers or gold-embossed assortments.

Each container is sealed to exclude air, moisture, and dust. For transit, jars are encased in protective cushioning and shipped inside heavy-duty corrugated cartons to prevent damage.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a tracking link via email or SMS. You can use this link to check real-time transit updates directly from our logistics partners.

If you possess an active member account, you can also view current order tracking status, logistics codes, and details inside your User Dashboard.

5. Delivery Restrictions

We deliver across most PIN codes in India. We do not support delivery to P.O. boxes or military post offices (APO/FPO). Certain remote geographical areas or territories under logistics restrictions may require additional delivery days.

If you require international shipping or bespoke courier commissions, please reach out directly to our concierge team at concierge@reinoro.com.
