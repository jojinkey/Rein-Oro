Returns & Refunds

Returns

1. Eligibility Criteria 2. Return Process 3. Refund Processing 4. Damages & Issues 5. Exchanges Policy

1. Eligibility Criteria

We pride ourselves on the exceptional quality of our gourmet dry fruits and makhanas. However, if you are not fully satisfied with your purchase, we are here to assist you.

To be eligible for a return or replacement, the item must meet the following criteria:

  • The return request must be submitted within 15 days of order delivery.
  • The item must be in its original packaging, unopened, and with the protective foil seal fully intact.
  • Because our products are premium agricultural foodstuffs, we cannot accept returns on items that have been opened, consumed, or exposed to external air and moisture.

2. Return Process

To initiate a return or replacement, please carry out the following instructions:

  1. Send an email to our concierge team at concierge@reinoro.com with your Order ID (e.g. RO-123456) and a brief description of the issue.
  2. If the return is due to shipping damage or product defect, please attach a photograph showing the sealed condition and packaging.
  3. Our team will verify the details within 24 hours. Upon approval, we will schedule a complimentary reverse pickup from your registered address.

3. Refund Processing

Once your returned item is received at our quality assurance facility, we will inspect it to confirm that the container seal remains unopened and undamaged. We will send you an email confirmation indicating whether the refund has been approved.

Upon approval, the refund will be processed and credited back using your original payment method:

  • UPI & Card Payments: Credited within 5 to 7 business days.
  • Net Banking & Wallets: Credited within 3 to 5 business days.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): You will be requested to provide bank account details for a secure bank transfer, completed within 5 business days.

4. Damages & Issues

Please inspect your purchase immediately upon delivery. If you notice that the outer carton is crushed, or if the product seal inside the box has broken during transit, do not consume the contents.

Damaged Transit Protection

We provide full insurance for transit damages. Contact our concierge desk within 48 hours of delivery with packaging pictures, and we will dispatch a free express replacement package immediately.

5. Exchanges Policy

We support exchanges for different flavors (e.g. exchanging Classic Salted Makhana for Peri Peri Makhana) provided the request is submitted within 15 days of delivery, the container is unopened, and the exchange item is of equal value.

If you wish to exchange an item, please contact our concierge team and we will coordinate the reverse pick-up and delivery schedule.
